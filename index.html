US Stock Tips (Education-based)

Short, practical ideas you can apply — with risk context included.

1) Don’t marry a ticker

Stocks move on expectations. Recheck thesis after earnings, guidance, or major news.

MindsetEarnings

2) Favor quality + cash flow

For long-term, prioritize strong margins, consistent revenue growth, and healthy balance sheets.

FundamentalsLong-term

3) ETFs for smoother learning

If you’re new, broad ETFs can reduce single-company risk while you build skills.

ETFsBeginner

4) Position sizing is the real “secret”

Even a great pick can hurt if size is too big. Use a max risk per trade rule.

RiskRules

5) Avoid “news FOMO” entries

After big headlines, price is often stretched. Wait for pullbacks or confirmation.

DisciplineMomentum

6) Write a plan before you buy

Entry, exit, time horizon, and what invalidates your thesis. No plan = vibes.
